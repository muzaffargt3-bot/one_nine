Our Story

One Nine Photography was born out of a deep passion for storytelling and a relentless pursuit of visual perfection. Located in the beautiful landscapes of Thundathumkadavu, Varapuzha, Kerala, we draw inspiration from the rich culture, vibrant colors, and authentic emotions of our surroundings.

We don't just take photographs; we craft cinematic experiences. For us, a wedding is more than an event—it's a collection of unrepeatable moments, fleeting glances, and profound promises. Our goal is to capture the essence of your love story with a touch of modern luxury.

The Team

We are a hand-picked team of passionate photographers, cinematographers, and editors. With over 130+ happy clients, our reputation is built on trust, creativity, and an uncompromising commitment to quality.

Our approach is unobtrusive yet highly attentive. We blend into the background to capture candid magic, while also gently guiding you for those breathtaking, editorial-style portraits.
